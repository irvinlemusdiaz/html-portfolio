The Best Movies According to Irvin!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avenger's Infinity War

The penultimate climax to the original Avenger's movies. Arguably better than End Game. Don't @ me.

The Dark Knight

This is the perfect follow up to the 2005 Batman Begins. This movie encapulates what a sequel should be and what other sequels wish they could be.

Star Wars Episode III

Hayden Chritensen plays an amazing fallen Anakin. Everthing from the John WIllaims score to the visual effects that still amaze to this day just scream "masterpiece"!
